About HackArena

Built by students, informed by research — designed to fix what the others got wrong.

🔒

Clean, Intuitive UI

Inspired by picoCTF's simplicity — no information overload. Every challenge is one click away with clear category tags and difficulty indicators.

🏭

Structured Learning Paths

We took TryHackMe's idea of guided paths and made them clearer. Each category has a recommended order and every challenge explains what skill you're building.

🥇

Real Gamification

Points, streaks, and leaderboards that actually mean something. Earned badges reflect real milestones — not just clicking "next."

Hints Without Handholding

Hints guide — they never solve. Our hints nudge you toward independent problem-solving, preserving the challenge and the learning.

Real-World Context

Every challenge explains why the skill matters. Not just "find the flag" — but here's where this vulnerability exists and why it's critical.

Beginner to Advanced

Difficulty scales naturally. Beginners start with clearly labeled entry-level challenges. No experience needed to get started.
